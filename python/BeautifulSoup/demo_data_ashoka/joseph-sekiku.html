JOSEPH SEKIKU

    Economic Development

Family Alliance for Development and Cooperation (FADECO)

Tanzania,
    Africa

Starting in northwestern Tanzania, Joseph Sekiku is improving economic possibilities for small-scale farmers by giving them greater post-production control over their products and greater access to global markets. By doing so, he equips farmers to make their own decisions about their business rather than be directed and exploited by middlemen.

This profile below was prepared when Joseph Sekiku was elected to the Ashoka Fellowship in 2007.
